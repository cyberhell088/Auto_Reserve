Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

You can browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details and place your order.

2. What payment methods are available?

We accept Cash on Delivery, Khalti, and eSewa payments.

3. How can I track my order?

After placing an order, go to the "Orders" page to see the status of your orders.

4. Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled before they are marked as "Preparing". Please contact us for cancellations.

5. Do you offer discounts or deals?

Yes, visit our "Offers" page to see the latest discounts and special deals.
